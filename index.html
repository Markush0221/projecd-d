TAKUMI FUJIWARA

The legendary "Ghost of Akina." Takumi serves as the Downhill Ace of Project D. Having spent five years delivering tofu in the early morning hours, he developed an instinctive mastery of weight distribution. He utilizes the "Gutter Run" technique and "Blind Attack" (turning off headlights) to psychologically shatter opponents in much faster cars.
