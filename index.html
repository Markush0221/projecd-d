Takumi Fujiwara (The Ghost of Akina) Takumi is the primary downhill specialist of Project D. Having honed his skills for years delivering tofu in his father's AE86, he possesses a supernatural ability to sense weight transfer and tire grip. His signature moves include the "Gutter Run" and the "Blind Attack" (driving without headlights). He is the heart of the team's winning streak.

Fourth Stage Battle Log

Battle Log #01Seven Star Leaf
Rival:Toru Suetsugu
Machine:Honda Roadster (S2000 Engine)
Location:Momiji Line (Downhill)
The Crash of Pride: Toru Suetsugu was a local hero who specialized in risky gutter runs. In the opening battle of Project D, he attempted to follow Takumi's line but pushed his lightweight Roadster too far, resulting in a spectacular flip-over crash.
Battle Log #02Todo School
Rival:Daiki Ninomiya
Machine:Honda Civic Type R (EK9)
Location:Happogahara (Downhill)
Precision vs. Instinct: Daiki was a top student of the Todo Racing School. He used professional FF techniques to minimize braking and maximize corner speed. Takumi won by braking later than logically possible, shaking Daiki's confidence.
Battle Log #03Todo School (Pro)
Rival:Tomoyuki Tachi
Machine:EK9 Civic (Spoon Demo Car)
Location:Happogahara (Downhill)
The Invisible Attack: Tomoyuki is a professional racer who returned to help his school. This was Takumi's toughest battle yet. To win, Takumi turned off his headlights (Blind Attack) to hide his position and overtake the professional.
Battle Log #04The Legend Returns
Rival:Bunta Fujiwara
Machine:Subaru Impreza WRX STi (GC8)
Location:Akina Pass
Family Rivalry: An impromptu battle on Akina. Bunta easily overtook Takumi in his new 4WD Impreza, showing Takumi that there is still a massive world of technical skill he has yet to master.
Battle Log #12Purple Shadow (God Foot)
Rival:Kozo Hoshino
Machine:Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34)
Location:Tsukuba (Uphill/Downhill)
Tire War: Hoshino, the "God Foot," drove a powerful R34 Skyline. He pushed Keisuke to his absolute limits in an endurance-style uphill/downhill race. Keisuke won because Hoshino's tires finally gave out after multiple rounds.
Battle Log #13Purple Shadow (God Hand)
Rival:Toshiya Joshima
Machine:Honda S2000
Location:Tsukuba (Downhill)
The Ultimate Lesson: Joshima, the "God Hand," is the ultimate downhill racer who steers with one hand. He was technically superior to Takumi, but lost the battle when he became physically ill from the heat and duration of the race, forcing him to pull over.
