The legendary delivery boy of Mount Akina. Takumi serves as the Downhill Ace of Project D. Kanto Track List
Course 01:Momiji Line (Tochigi) Momiji Line (もみじライン Momiji Rain) is the home course of Seven Star Leaf. It is located in Nikko, Tochigi. This is where Project D's first battle in the Tochigi Prefecture is held, notably where Toru attempts to use Takumi's lidless gutter shortcut technique only to fail in doing so.
Course 02:Happogahara (Todo Base) Happogahara (八方ヶ原) is the home course of the Todo School. It is located in Nasushiobara, Tochigi. This is where Takumi Fujiwara battles professional driver Tomoyuki Tachi in Fourth Stage.
Course 03:Sadamine Pass (Saitama) Sadamine Pass (定峰峠, Sadamine Touge) is first home course of the Northern Saitama Alliance, It is located east of Chichibu, Saitama, north of Shomaru Pass and east of Tsuchisaka. The higher elevation portion of the pass is actually the Shiroishi Pass Oku-Musashi Green Line, which then connects to the actual Sadamine Pass.
Course 04:Tsukuba (Purple Shadow Territory) Tsukuba (筑波) is the home course of Purple Shadow. It is located at the center of Tsukuba, Ibaraki. Purple Shadow's race is held at Mount Tsukuba's Fruits Line, which spans Oshito, Tsuchiura and Shibauchi, Ishioka.
